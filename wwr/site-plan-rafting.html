Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to spread awareness of the company and the adventures it offers across Northern California.

Audience

The target audience for this website are thrill seekers between the age of 18 and 35. This website is for people looking for an exciting adventure and an experience in nature What do they want? They want an exciting experience they will remember for years. They want to be able to tell exciting stories to their friends and family. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find the locations and routes of our adventures as well as pricing information. How will my audience usually access my site? They will most often access the site on laptops and desktops for in depth research but will also have brief searches on mobile devices.
